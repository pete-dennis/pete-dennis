Pete Dennis

about

Pete Dennis is a musician, improviser, composer, producer, engineer, coder and sonic researcher They are a queer science fiction futurist who lives in West Philadelphia. They love playing in the band Spectral Forces and collaborating with artists like Mitamu, their partner Abigail Swoboda, St. John's Wort, Julia Pratt, and Cutting Ceremony among others. They studied with the great William Parker, David Wong, Mark Helias, and Ben Schachter Music is for everyone, if it isn't for everyone, then it isn't music.
